Robbers In Disguise

GamePlay Video :

Pitch :

Put on your costume and go on a heist in unusual places with your friends!

Progress through puzzles, traps, and unexpected situations, using coordination and dexterity to complete your mission!

Description :

"Robbers in Disguise" is a cooperative party game for 4 players, featuring multiple action-packed phases with an absurd tone. The mechanics are mainly based on physics and the environment. It is played with a controller on an undivided screen.

Tool :

During the research phase, we developed a Unity tool to help the level artists and designers iterate more easily, work with multiple prefabs at the same time, and snap prefabs to whatever they want.

Active Ragdoll :

Firstly, we tried to create a character with physics by using rigidbodies. Rigidbodies are directly controlled by the physics engine to simulate the behavior of physical objects. We also used joints, which allow us to connect a rigidbody to another or define a fixed point in space. Joints apply a force that moves the rigidbody and limit or restrict its movements.

However, we encountered some issues when trying to imitate the animator's animations, where our character's movements were completely overridden and became rigid. To find an alternative solution, we decided to copy the animations of a doll hidden somewhere in the map. By doing so, our character could add the local rotations of all its limbs to its own, which gave it a more realistic feel while still keeping the animations that were given to us.

As you can see just above, we had some issues with the chicken when we wanted it to be attracted by a giant magnet. But now, as professionals in manipulating physics, we were able to fix the problem.

EgoRush

Concept :

The game concept was a first person "Runner" game where people will try to reach the end as fast as possible. The more they try, the more they can optimize the path to the end.

Mechanics :

The character controller is in first person, ZQSD (French keyboard / Temporary) to move, Space/Scroll wheel to jump and the mouse to move the camera arround.

Our game allows you to Wall Ride, and to jump from them, we also have creeps that the player can grip on and jump a lot higher than from the ground.

You can respawn whenever you want with the Key "R" to retry if you failed a jump or just want to improve a specific part.

Feeling :

We really want our game to be "Fluid" while playing, so we worked a lot on the camera and Feedbacks of the movement of the player. Like the camera leans when a player is wallrunning, or lerp toward the same forward than the player, to give a sensation of smoothness while wallrunning.

Flatten Penguin

Pitch :

You play as a Penguin who has the ability to flatten himself from 3 dimensions to 2 dimensions. You will use this power to valiantly explore ancient Aztec ruins in search of the Doggly Hot God.

Context :

This game was made in 48 hours by 5 people during the Perpignan Game Jam 2022.

Experience :

It was a great opportunity to work with my group on this game jam. I learned a lot from them, whether it was their work style or way of thinking. It was also very interesting to understand how to optimize time and manage workload within the given timeframe.
